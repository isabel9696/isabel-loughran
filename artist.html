Isabel Loughran creates a sensory experience with her ceramic forms. Born in 1996 in Dublin, she achieved a First Class Honors Degree in International BA (Hons) Fine Art in The National College of Art and Design. Her forms are designed for the sensory channels, specifically touch and hearing. Her motivation lies in reminding the viewer where our experience begins, the body.
